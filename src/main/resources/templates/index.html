Schedule

- online tool to plan your day!
Create long-term tasks or divide your day into temporary activities.

    Make plans by creating Tasks. There is no need to keep everything in your head, so write down all that you want to do! Be it a hobby, travelling plan or career ambitions - put it into a note, so that you know what to do and pursue your goals.
     Set priorities for what is important to you. The deadlines knocking at your door and some tasks can wait in favor of others. Mark them with corresponding numbers to see what has to be done first.
     Complete your tasks and review them on the History page to see your progress. And don't forget that planning is one thing, but making your plans real is wholly up to you!




    Manage your time using Activities. Whether shopping for groceries or taking your pet for a walk, add it to your daily schedule and see when you have some time left for yourself.
    Activities are meant to help you structure your day and remind you of certain things. For example, regularly watering flowers or cleaning the house can easily be forgotten. Therefore, you can create repeating activities that will appear on your daily schedule to remind you of your chores.
